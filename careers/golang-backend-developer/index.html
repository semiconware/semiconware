Backend Developer (Go)

About Us:

At Semiconware, we are at the forefront of innovation, committed to developing cutting-edge solutions that make a real difference. We are a team of passionate, driven individuals who thrive in a dynamic and collaborative environment. Our work impacts millions of users worldwide, and we are looking for a talented Backend Developer with a strong background in Go to join our team.

Job Description:

As a Backend Developer at Semiconware, you will be part of a team responsible for the development and maintenance of our backend services. You will play a crucial role in creating high-performance, scalable, and reliable solutions to meet our business needs. With a focus on the Go programming language, you will work closely with our development team to design, implement, and deploy backend services that support our applications and platforms.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Design, develop, and maintain efficient, reusable, and reliable Go code.
  • Implement performance and quality modules to ensure the responsiveness and scalability of applications.
  • Identify bottlenecks and bugs, and devise solutions to mitigate and address these issues.
  • Collaborate with the frontend team to integrate user-facing elements with server-side logic.
  • Contribute to the entire application lifecycle, focusing on coding, debugging, and testing.
  • Participate in code reviews to maintain high code quality standards.
  • Stay updated with emerging technologies and industry trends to incorporate into operations and practices.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
  • Minimum of 3 years of experience as a Backend Developer.
  • Strong proficiency in any language Python, Java or Go, with a good knowledge of its ecosystems.
  • Experience with RESTful APIs and microservices architecture.
  • Familiarity with SQL/NoSQL databases, their declarative query languages, and ORM frameworks.
  • Understanding of fundamental design principles behind a scalable application.
  • Proficient understanding of code versioning tools, such as Git.
  • Ability to work independently and in a team, with excellent communication skills.
  • Problem-solving mindset and a passion for learning new technologies.

Nice to Have:

  • Experience with Docker and Kubernetes.
  • Familiarity with continuous integration and deployment (CI/CD) pipelines.
  • Knowledge of cloud services (AWS, GCP, Azure) and their managed services.

What We Offer:

  • Competitive salary and benefits package.
  • Flexible working hours and remote work options.
  • A dynamic and supportive work environment.
  • Opportunities for professional growth and development.
  • Access to cutting-edge technologies and tools.

How to Apply:

Please submit your resume, along with a cover letter and any relevant project/portfolio links to career@semiconware.com. In your cover letter, explain why you are a good fit for this role and how your skills and experiences will contribute to our team.

Semiconware is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees.
