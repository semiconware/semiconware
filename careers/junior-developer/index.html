About Us:

Semiconware Technologies Pvt Ltd is a product development company that specializes in creating innovative solutions with focus on Automotive Industries. Our team is at the forefront of technology, leveraging the power of Golang, Rust, Angular, Postgres, and Git to build scalable, high-performance applications. We are committed to excellence and are looking for a passionate Junior Software Developer to join our dynamic team.

Job Description:

As a Junior Software Developer at Semiconware Technologies Pvt Ltd, you will have the opportunity to work on exciting projects alongside a team of experienced developers. You will contribute to the development, testing, and maintenance of our software products, ensuring they meet our high standards of quality and performance. This role is a fantastic opportunity for a motivated fresher who is eager to learn and grow in a supportive and innovative environment.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Participate in the entire application lifecycle, focusing on coding, debugging, and testing.
  • Contribute to the development of back-end services with Golang and Rust.
  • Assist in developing front-end interfaces with Angular.
  • Work with Postgres to implement and manage database solutions.
  • Utilize Git for version control and collaboration with the development team.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features.
  • Ensure the performance, quality, and responsiveness of applications.
  • Identify and correct bottlenecks and fix bugs.
  • Help maintain code quality, organization, and automatization.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
  • Strong foundation in computer science, with competencies in data structures, algorithms, and software design.
  • Fundamentals of any programming language e.g. Python, Java, Golang, Rust.
  • Basic knowledge of SQL (Postgres)
  • Basic knowledge of Git
  • Passion for learning new technologies and programming languages.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Ability to work effectively in a team as well as independently.
  • Good communication skills, both written and verbal.
  • A proactive attitude and the ability to take initiative.

What We Offer:

  • A vibrant and inclusive workplace culture.
  • Opportunities for professional growth and development.
  • Exposure to cutting-edge technologies and projects.
  • A supportive environment where creativity and innovation are encouraged.
  • Competitive salary and benefits package.

How to Apply:

Please send your resume, along with a cover letter and any relevant project/portfolio links, to career@semiconware.com. In your resume, please highlight your interest in the role, your relevant skills/experiences, and why you believe you are the right fit for Semiconware Technologies Pvt Ltd.

Semiconware Technologies Pvt Ltd is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees.
